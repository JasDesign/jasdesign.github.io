EatJoy

Baby Utensil Design for Picky Eaters

  • 1 in 3 toddlers are picky eaters
  • Average spends 45min to 1.5hr to finish a meal
  • Picky eating usually last for 2 years

Picky Eater Problem

Solution

  • Positive encouragement helps develop good habits
  • Interactive toy captures attention and improves brain and sensory development
  • Feel fun to eat

Reason for Picky Eating

Internal Factors

 

  • Not hungry
  • Dislike food
  • Want to show independence
  • Feel uncomfortable

External Factors

 

  • Pressure from parents
  • Draw interests to other things

Can be solved by design

Existing Products for Picky Eaters

  • Focus on playing over eating
  • Easy to get messy
  • Extend meal time
  • Unable to help develop a good eating manner

Why Spoon?

  • The most direct utensil to baby
  • Easy to catch attention
  • Helps develop utensil skills
  • Encourages independence
  • For both liquid and solid food
  • Portable
  • Easy to clean
