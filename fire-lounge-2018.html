Product Details

Dimensions: H 46“   W 79”   D 75“    Seat - D 61”  H 17“

Materials: Glass; wooden structure; polyurethane upholstery foam covered with printed velvet fabric and full grain leather.

The contours of the Fire Lounge are beautifully sleek with an upward flow, which impart a sense of sublimation. Rounded platform and soft velvet cushion create a milder, relaxed tone with the Fire Lounge. The animated brush stroke art gives this lounge an artistic flair with visual contrast that will enrich any living room decor. Two dynamic glass screens form an elegant frame for the flame and embrace the round platform as a seat back. The "floating" white leather cushion bars on the glass are comfortable for the back, and also provide visibility to the flame. The classic black & white design enhances the minimalist profile and sets off the beauty of fire.

The concept for Fire Lounge began with the idea of fusing fire with seating to create a lively and romantic ambiance for relaxation. The visually mesmerizing, alluring and irresistible flame makes the Fire Lounge a paradise to sit, lay and enjoy wine.

Fire Lounge

Fire Lounge has a simulated faux fire system that uses water vapour and specialty lighting to create realistic fire effects. Now you can enjoy the dramatic impact of fire, without worrying about the safety issues associated with real flame. The seating area can also be heated as you like. Fire Lounge provides comfiness with stunning ambiance for all seasons.
